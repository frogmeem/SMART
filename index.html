About our project

We want to improve the dining experience on campus. Currently, the food options can be underwhelming, both in taste and presentation. Our team is dedicated to enhancing the appeal of campus dining, making mealtimes a more enjoyable experience for everyone. We're committed to collaborating with students, staff, and dining services to create a dining experience that exceeds expectations.
